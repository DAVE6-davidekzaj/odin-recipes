Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients, excluding quail and vegetable oil, in large heavy-duty zip-top plastic bag and add quail. Seal, and chill 30 minutes, turning occasionally.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 F degrees.
  3. Roast quail in the oven uncovered for 20 to 30 minutes or until quail is done.
  4. In the meantime, pour the marinade in a small pot and simmer over medium heat for 10 minutes, it should reduce a bit.
  5. Pour marinade sauce over quail and serve hot with your favorite side dish.
